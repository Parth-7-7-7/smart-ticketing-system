Billing Issues:
- Failed payments can occur due to expired cards.
- Invoices are generated 1st of every month.
- Refunds take 5–7 business days.
- Refunds are issued to the original payment method.

Account Issues:
- Password reset emails may take a few minutes.
- Accounts can be locked after multiple failed logins.
- If a user cannot access their account, they should reset their password.
- Password reset links expire after 15 minutes. 

Technical Issues:
- App crashes can be fixed by restarting the service.
- Clear browser cache for UI issues.

General:
- Support hours are 9 AM – 6 PM IST.
- Paid users receive priority support.
